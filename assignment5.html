Courtenay's Assignment 4

This project involves the following two parts:

Part 1: Mesh exploration: For online inspiration, start by visiting Thingiverse or a comparable website and look for examples of other peoples' creations, e.g., STLs and interesting closed meshes. Then modify the mesh of two STLs to produce a single printable STL. Before slicing for and printing, confirm that the mesh passes all checks for rapid prototyping.
Part 2: Lamp creation: Using measurements of the lamp innards that I purchased on Amazon.com as a point of departure, create a lamp that can be assembled around the lamp's innards using a variety a digital fabrication techniques.
