Courtenay's Assignment 4

This project involves the following two parts:

Part 1: Mesh exploration: Modify the mesh of two STLs to produce a single printable STL. For online inspiration, start by visiting Thingiverse or a comparable site to look for examples of other peoples' creations, e.g., STLs and closed meshes, to play with for learning and experimentation purposes. Once I create a new mesh, before exporting the STL from Rhino, I need to confirm that it passed all checks for rapid prototyping.
Part 2: Lamp creation: Using measurements of the lamp innards that I purchased on Amazon.com as a point of departure, create a lamp that can be assembled around the innards using a variety a digital fabrication techniques.
